About the Complexity

Yes, these demos use advanced mathematics (topology, differential geometry, etc). But you don't need to understand the math to appreciate what you're seeing.

Like watching birds fly - you don't need aerodynamics equations to feel the beauty of their movement. The math is there for those who want it, but the experience is for everyone.

What Am I Looking At?

  • The rotating shapes represent ideas or thoughts
  • Their connections show how concepts relate
  • The colors indicate different types of mental energy
  • The movement follows actual physics laws, but for consciousness
